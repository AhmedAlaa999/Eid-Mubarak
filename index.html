What is Eid? 🌙

Eid is one of the most important celebrations in Islam. Muslims around the world celebrate it with prayer, food, and family.

There are two Eids. Eid al-Fitr is celebrated after Ramadan ends, and Eid al-Adha is the Festival of Sacrifice.

Eid Traditions ✨

  1. Wake up early and pray Eid prayer
  2. Wear new clothes
  3. Visit family and friends
  4. Give gifts and Eidi to children
  5. Pay Zakat
